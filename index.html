Exploring World Population
1970-2020

This visualisation explores the population of all countries and territories in the world between 1970 to 2020.
The dataset which were used to create this visualisation can be found via this link.

POPULATION DENSITY

The population density for each country or territory is calculated by dividing population by area size. The area size is measered in square kilometers.






TOTAL POPULATION

In 2020, the total population in the world was 7.75 billion. Of the 195 countries in the world, more than half of our population resided in only 7 countries

GROWTH RATE

China is currently the most populous country in the world. However, the growth rate of China has been declining, whereas India's population is projected to continue rising till 2050 and eventually outrank China as the most populous country.
